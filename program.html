Let's Donate To Needy People For Better Lives

Why Donate?

Children are the foundation of our country. It is crucial to ensure that all children have access to quality education, proper nutrition, and a safe environment, so they can reach their full potential. Therefore, Human Help Life Foundation has been working towards helping children achieve their dreams and providing them with the childhoods they truly deserve.

Our Work:

We operate 10 projects in 5 states and 80 districts, including Mumbai, Pune, Bangalore, Kolkata, and Chennai. We collaborate with grassroots project partners to address the pressing needs of children and bring about long-lasting change. Our work focuses on three key areas:

Education:

We conduct awareness drives to highlight the importance of education and ensure that all children are enrolled in school.

Protection:

We prevent child marriage and child labor by creating awareness about the harmful effects of these practices. Additionally, we connect families with MNERGA job opportunities.

Health and Nutrition:

We ensure children receive timely vaccinations and sufficient nutrition by connecting them to government healthcare services.

Every contribution you make helps us ensure that India’s children can attend school, receive proper nutrition and healthcare, and remain protected from abuse and exploitation. Join us in creating happier childhoods for India’s children.
